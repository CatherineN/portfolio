Wegmans Meals2GO Usability Testing

Project Length: September 2022 - December 2022

Role: Team Lead

Activities: Heuristic Evaluation, Experiment Design, User Testing


Overview:

As part of HCI Usability Testing course within RIT's program, student teams worked with local clients to provide heuristic evaluations and user testing for their products. Our team collaborated with Wegmans to evaluate and test their Meals 2GO app. Discussed pain points with the design team, conducted individual heuristic evaluations, then designed a usability test that was run early December. Results from the test were compiled and presented to the client in December.
