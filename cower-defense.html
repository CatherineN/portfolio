Cower Defense (September 2017)

" The year is 3420ATE (After The End), and the boys of the Creamy Way Galaxy in the far reaches of space are under the rule of their evil cow overlords. For decades, the boys have plotted a means of overthrowing and escape. They meet in secret, gathering the finest among their ranks to bring an end to their bovine oppressors.

Then finally, through a stroke of genius and a lot of willpower, the cowboys (as they are called) developed anti-cow turrets. They planned on launching an attack against their rulers, however in the 11th hour, tragedy struck!

Because one of the turrets had been set up incorrectly, the internal drive divided by zero, causing the turret to implode in upon itself and produce a wormhole. The cowboys were sucked into this wormhole and ended up in the countryside of 2017’s Wyoming.

It was in Wyoming that they learned that cows would not oppress them here, but rather they could command their own armies of cows. And with their first introduction to milk and manure, it wasn’t long before the squabbling over resources and territory began.

And thus began the deadly struggle of boys and their cows, fighting over poo... "


A 3-4 player party game with battle royale and tower defense elements. A 5 week long project. I handled card creation, lore, and worked with the team during weekly playtesting sessions.

Playtesting was conducted once a week. I logged the behaviors of players and their responses to the cards, game flow, and their comprehension of the rule guide. Taking the findings from the testing, the team would revise the gameplay and rules.
